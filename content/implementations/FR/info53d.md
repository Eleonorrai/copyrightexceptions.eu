- any user

- use in analyses and as short quotations

- works
- performances
- phonograms
- film fixations
- broadcasts
- press publications
compensation:
- no compensation required
attribution: 
- the name of the author and the source must be clearly stated
otherConditions: 
- works used must be already disclosed
