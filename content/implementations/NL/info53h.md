weight: 58
exceptions:
- info53h
jurisdictions:
- NL
score: 2
description: "Exception allowing the reproduction or making available of representations of works of visual art and architecture which were made for permanent placement in public places." 
beneficiaries:
- any user
purposes: 
- any purpose 
usage:
- reproduction
- making available
subjectmatter:
- drawings, paintings, construction and sculptures, lithography, engraving and other sheet metal works
- designs, sketches and plastic works relating to architecture, geography, local history or other sciences
compensation:
- not required
attribution: 
- not required
otherConditions: 
- the work needs to be made made to be permanently placed in public places
- the works needs to be presented in the context where the object is found
- where the reproductions are included in a compilation work, no more than a few of the same creator's works may be copied 
remarks: "The exception does not include the right to make derivative works. "
